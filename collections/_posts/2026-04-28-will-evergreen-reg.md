[Will Rourk](/people/will-rourk) directed a team of students, Fluvanna County community, & architecture preservation professionals in [completing the documentation necessary to nominate](https://bsky.app/profile/scholarslab.bsky.social/post/3mkkeymn4vs25) the ~1935 Evergreen African-American School as an official historic site with the Virginia Dept of Historic Resources.
