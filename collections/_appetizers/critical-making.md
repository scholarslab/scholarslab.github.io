1. [Association of Creative Technologies in Academic Libraries](https://actalibraries.org): holds an annual conference  
2. [Higher Education Makerspaces Initiatives](https://hemi-makers.org) (HEMI): a collaborative of leading universities focused on solving the challenges of academic makerspaces and making their combined learnings available to others; holds an annual conference  
3. [Hackaday](https://hackaday.com ) "serves up Fresh Hacks Every Day from around the Internet. Our playful posts are the gold-standard in entertainment for engineers and engineering enthusiasts"
4. [#DHmakes feed on Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/literaturegeek.bsky.social/feed/aaadokeexl2vo) & ["What is #DHMakes?"](https://tinyurl.com/whatisDHmakes): supportive community & collaboration for craft, making, and art by DH, GLAM, and knowledge-work folks (including work both scholarly and not)  
5. [Zine Bakery's catalog section on creative building](https://airtable.com/appY7WyBFjSzLXQd6/shrXhMUlZa80tCL5r): Info & links to (usually free) zines on crafting, making, and art
