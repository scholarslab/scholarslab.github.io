Applications for the Graduate Fellowship in Digital Humanities are due soon! The application deadline for Graduate Fellowships in Digital Humanities to be held in the Scholars’ Lab during the 2025-2026 academic year is February 15th, 2025.

If you’re interested in learning more about the fellowship or have questions, please consider attending the information session - January 15, 2025 from 1:00-2:00PM on Zoom. Please [register to attend](https://cal.lib.virginia.edu/event/13365703). You are, of course, welcome to write to [Brandon Walsh](mailto:bmw9t@virginia.edu) separately and/or sooner for an individual meeting to discuss your application so that you can get working. The full CFP can be found [here](https://scholarslab.lib.virginia.edu/digital-humanities-fellows/).
