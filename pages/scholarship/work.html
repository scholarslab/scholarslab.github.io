Here we share a sampling of the Scholars' Lab scholarship in its many states and forms.

Given an increased emphasis on evolving, collaborative, logo-less work—variously shaped collaborations, consultations, community-building, teaching, advanced mentorship, and deep advising on projects owned by others—we’re working to add many more examples of that full spectrum of scholarship here. We don't list all this work here as we don't want this page’s design to seem to claim ownership for work where we’ve been advisors, participants, or made other contributions not accurately represented by simply listing the name of a project and a logo.

In the meantime, we encourage you to reach out to ScholarsLab@Virginia.edu to share your areas of interest-we'll reply with examples of the kind of relevant work we've been doing here! With 11 full-time experts in many methods and fields, and a rich interdisciplinary community, we've likely worked in your area of interest (or can connect you to those who do).
